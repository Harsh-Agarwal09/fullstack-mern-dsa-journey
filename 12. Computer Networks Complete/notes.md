Computer Networks

# Network -> It is a group or system of interconnected people or items. Computers connected with each other with cables or wireless is called computer networks.

# Internet -> In a nutshell, internet is a network of computer networks. Complex web of interconnected computer networks.

History Of Internet

In 1957 Soviet Union launced its first satellite called sputnik
In 1957 United States launced its first satellite called ARPA(Advanced Research Project Agency)
In 1960's - 1970's ARPA launched communication system for ARPA's computer to talk
In 1969 ARPA launched ARPANET
In 1980's they started transforming everything to TCP/IP protocol. At this time ARPANET was start calling INTERNET
